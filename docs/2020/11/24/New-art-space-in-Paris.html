New art space in paris

24 Nov 2020 - Kate

The time when all the galleries stay closed I had a chance to expose my artworks in the marvellous office space at 29 Bd. Exelmans Paris 75016. 7 artworks are represented where including the flagship '2020' painting.
For the best experince, take a RDV with me, as I can't always be present in the gallery. All my contacts are listed at the bottom of the page.
Othewise, the company director Laurent will be happy to show you the collection.

Open every Mon-Sat 10-19h .
From 24 Nov to 24 Dec 2020.
