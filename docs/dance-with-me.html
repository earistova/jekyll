Danse with Me!

The artwork collection originally presented to public at the exposition in 'Gallery 21' in Paris back in July 2016. Entirely materialised after living models at the stage of the mythical 'Academie de la Grande Chaumiere' in Paris. How time flies!

As you can see, most of the artworks are now in the private collections. All of them proved to be impossible to reproduce. In case you fell in love with one of the sold pieces, I'll be happy to propose a high-quality poster with author signature. Please contact.
Love, Kate
