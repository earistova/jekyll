Art-gallery 29 Bd. Exelmans Paris 75016

The time when all the galleries stay closed I had a chance to expose my artworks in the marvellous office space at 29 Bd. Exelmans Paris 75016. 7 artworks are represented where including the flagship '2020' painting.
For better experince, take a RDV with Kate, as she is not always present in the gallery. Use Contacts section in the bottom of the page.
Open every Mon-Sat 10-19h.
From 24 Nov to 24 Dec 2020.



Virtual gallery in 3D

Due to covid restrictions we organized the full exposition in a Virtual gallery online in 3D, so that everyone on the planet could see it without the need to travel to Paris.
When Artsteps website is loaded, simply click on 'Play' button to get automatic tour. Otherwise you can travel through the gallery space my selecting your position on the floor by mouse or even click on the artwork, so the camera will be centered at work.
Beware of suboptimal performance on mobile devices.
